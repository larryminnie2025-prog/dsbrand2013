The Beginning

DS Brand was built on a simple conviction — faith and discipline are inseparable. Born in 2013, this brand exists for the people who are in the gym at 5am and in the Word before the world wakes up.

Inspired by Romans 14:8 — "If we live, we live for the Lord" — every piece DS Brand creates is a statement. Not just clothing. A declaration of purpose.

The Crusader imagery is identity. A warrior who moves with conviction, trains with intention, and lives without compromise.
